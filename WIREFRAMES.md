## Design Principles

1. **Minimal & Fast**: Clean UI, no clutter
2. **Mobile-First**: Optimized for mobile screens (Farcaster Frame context)
3. **Clear CTAs**: Large, obvious buttons
4. **Immediate Feedback**: Instant visual feedback on actions
5. **Status Indicators**: Clear status colors (green/yellow/red)
6. **Consistent Colors**: Blue (primary), Purple (pools), Yellow (leaderboard), Green (success)

---

## Color Scheme

- **Primary**: Blue (#2563eb) - Main actions
- **Secondary**: Purple (#9333ea) - Prize pools
- **Success**: Green (#16a34a) - Correct answers
- **Error**: Red (#dc2626) - Wrong answers
- **Warning**: Yellow (#eab308) - Leaderboard/badges
- **Background**: Gradient from blue-900 to purple-900

---

## Typography

- **Headings**: Bold, large (2xl-5xl)
- **Body**: Regular, readable (base-lg)
- **Monospace**: Wallet addresses, times
- **Icons**: Emoji for visual interest

---

## Responsive Breakpoints

- Mobile: < 640px (primary)
- Tablet: 640px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px (secondary)
